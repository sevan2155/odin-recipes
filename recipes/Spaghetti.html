Spaghetti

This is a tasty spaghetti recipe for the soul

A tasty and hearty spaghetti recipe that is sure to warm your body while being cheap and quick to make. Surprise your guests with this amazing wonderful recipe fit for a king or queen. Never feel like you don't know what to cook for your family ever again. This recipe will surely become one of your go to recipes, a reoccuring wonderful memory for all those who partake.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Black Pepper
  • Basil
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Oregano
  • Olive Oil

Steps and Directions

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. Cook the sauce
  3. Add seasoning into the sauce
  4. Add olive oil to a hot pan
  5. Put the pasta and sauce into the pan
  6. Plate the food
  7. Top with cheese
