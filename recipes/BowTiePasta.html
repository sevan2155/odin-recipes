Bow Tie Pasta

Amazing Bow Tie Pasta Recipe

A affordable and quick bow tie pasta recipe. Great for any occasion or event! Enjoy this with family and or friends for a great meal that everyone would enjoy. No more needing to stress over what to make when this is available at any time you need.

This is a very versatile dish easy to change and substitute to your preferences.

Ingredients

  • Bow Tie Pasta
  • Your preferred pasta sauce
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Preferred seasoning(s)
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and pepper
  1. Boil pasta in water
  2. Add olive oil to pan
  3. Put cooked pasta into pan with sauce
  4. Add herbs
  5. Add seasoning(s)
  6. Plate
  7. Enjoy your meal!
