Chicken Stir Fry Recipe

Great Chicken Stir Fry Recipe

Tasty chicken stir fry recipe thats easy to make and also quick and affordable. Fun for all and enjoyable for even more! Try this out today for a fun lunch or dinner with your loved ones! Very versatile dish fit for a king!

Super fun time making this and will be a great addition to your roster of dishes. Don't know what to make for dinner? Try this dish out for a change! Might enjoy it so much you want more.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Veggies
  • Soy Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Sugar
  • Oil

Steps and Directions

  1. Chop chicken into pieces
  2. Wash, prep, and chop veggies
  3. Heat pan with oil
  4. Cook chicken and veggies in oil
  5. Add sauces, salt, and pepper
  6. Plate
  7. Enjoy
